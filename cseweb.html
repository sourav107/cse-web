About BUP — CSE

🏛️ Department Overview

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP) is a premier technology department committed to producing world-class computing professionals.

With our motto "Code · Compete · Conquer", we foster innovation, critical thinking, and technical excellence.

Our curriculum bridges theoretical knowledge with practical industry demands, preparing graduates for leadership roles in the global tech ecosystem.
